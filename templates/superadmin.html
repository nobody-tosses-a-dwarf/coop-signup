Email Templates

Platform-wide defaults for all outgoing emails. Co-op admins can override the member signup email from their own dashboard — their customisation takes priority over what's set here. Editing a field and saving replaces the default; clearing a field and saving reverts to the built-in fallback.

Sent daily or weekly to co-ops with digest notifications enabled. The member table and dashboard link are generated automatically from live data — only the intro paragraph above the table is editable here. Leave blank to use the default "N new members signed up in the past 24 hours."

{period_label}{count}{coop_name}
